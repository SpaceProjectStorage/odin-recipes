VEGGIE ROAST

Description

This one is simple, but allows for variation as to which vegetables used once you've done it a few times.

Ingredients

  • 1x Medium sized potato
  • 1-2 Carrots
  • 1x Head of broccoli
  • 1x Capsicum
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Tuscan seasoning
  • Spinach
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Slivered almonds

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C.
  2. Chop the potato and broccoli into approx 1cm cubes. Chop the capsicum slightly larger and carrot slightly smaller.
  3. Place the chopped veggies on a baking paper lined tray and cover with oil and tuscan seasoning. Toss the veggies to help cover with seasoning.
  4. Place into the oven when up to temperature. Start a timer for 20 minutes.
  5. While the veggies are in the oven, chop the cherry tomatoes in half. Mix into a bowl with the spinach. Add a splash of oil and balsamic vinegar.
  6. After the 20 miuntes is up, take the veggies out of the oven and turn over in the tray. Return to the oven for another 10 minutes.
  7. Take the veggies out of the oven and let sit for a couple minutes to cool down. Serve with the salad.
