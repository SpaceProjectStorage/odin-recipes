FISH AND CHIPS

Description

This is a good one for a Friday after work when you want something easy to help you wind down for the weekend. Minimal effort is needed, and it doesn't take too long!

Ingredients

  • Frozen fish from the store
  • Frozen chips from the store
  • Your choice of sauce

It's that simple!

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven on fan bake setting to 180C
  2. Put your portions of frozen fish & chips on a tray lined with baking paper
  3. Put the tray into the oven and start a timer for 20 minutes.
  4. Turn over the fish and chips. Return to the over for another 5 - 15 minutes depending on how crispy you want your food, or how hungry you are!
