TOFU RICE BOWLS

Description

Feeling like some healthy take-out but don't want to leave home? This one is deceptively easy and could be considered the entry level for meals to impress guests!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 350g Tofu
  • 1x Egg
  • Kewpie sesame sauce

Plus your choice of:

  • 1/2x Red onion
  • 1x Carrot
  • Frozen or fresh edamame
  • Handfull of Mushrooms
  • 1x Chillie
  • Handfull of Coriander

Method

  1. Place 1 cup of rice and 2 cups of water into a pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer and start a timer for 10 minutes. Then remove from the heat and sit for another 10 minutes
  2. While the rice is cooking crack the egg into a pan on medium heat. Break the yoke as is goes in and mix it into the egg white.
  3. While the egg is cooking chop the next items to cook depending on your final ingredient selection.
  4. Start cooking the next ingredient. Place the egg on a chopping board and cut it into approx. 0.5cm squares. The exact size and shape does not matter.
  5. Chop the final raw ingredients or grate the carrot. As each ingredient is completed, place it neatly by itself in the serving location.
  6. Chop the chillies, corriander, or other topping and let your guests know the meal is almost ready
  7. Serve with the rice first and allow each guest to portion their own cooked and raw ingredients. Finally douse with Kewpie Sesame sauce. Don't be shy!
